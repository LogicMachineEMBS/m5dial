# M5Dial mounting plate for electrical box

In our demonstration, we used ABB Niessen panels. <br>

2CLA867100A3001 - 8671 CB - White Glass <br>
2CLA867100A3101 - 8671 CN - Black Glass <br>

Avoid aluminum panels, as they disrupt the RFID functionality. <br>

We have also attached the STL file for 3D printing the inserts used to mount the panel to the electrical box. <br>
